About Us

Infinite Solar Power Solution is a leading solar solution provider in the Solar Industry in india. The Company is ISO 9001 : 2015 Certeficate, CE Certeficate , MSME Certeficate Holder & Udyog Aadhaar Registered company and in the business of manufacturing Solar Street Light Luminaries, Solar Pump Controller, Solar Street Light System,and Solar Power Plant. At present, when the world is suffering from the problems of shortage in power, we have come out with innovative solutions of solving the shortages by supplying a wide range of solar power products such as solar panel , solar luminaries ,solar power plant , solar equipment,.We deal in marketing of the same in various part of India such as Murshidabad, Nadia, Birbhum, Maldha and other districts, Assam, Tripura and other north eastern states,Jharkhand, Bihar and Orissa etc. Our technical team always developing innovative products of solar power solutions ,which makes us one of the best solar company in West Bengal and North eastern parts of India. Our company provides an unique opportunity to individuals and businesses from all over eastern India .
